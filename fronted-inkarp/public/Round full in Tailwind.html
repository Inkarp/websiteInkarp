To round fully (make something a full circle) in Tailwind CSS, use the class:

sql
rounded-full

This applies the maximum border-radius, which is perfect for turning square elements into circles—like avatars or buttons—as long as the width and height are equal.

Example:

jsx
<div className="w-16 h-16 bg-blue-500 rounded-full"></div>

That gives you a blue circle.

Absolutely! Here's a breakdown of how rounded-full works and more ways you can use it in Tailwind:

🔵 1. Basic Circle

To make a circle, the key is equal width and height:

html
<div className="w-24 h-24 bg-green-500 rounded-full"></div>

🖼️ 2. Circle Image (Avatar)

jsx
<img
  src="https://via.placeholder.com/150"
  className="w-16 h-16 rounded-full object-cover"
/>

  • rounded-full – makes it a circle.

  • object-cover – makes sure the image fills the container without distortion.

🧠 3. Round Buttons

jsx
<button className="w-12 h-12 bg-blue-600 text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center">
  +
</button>

This gives you a circular button with a centered +.

🟢 4. With Borders or Shadows

html
<div className="w-20 h-20 rounded-full border-4 border-green-500 shadow-lg"></div>

Add border, shadow, or even ring utilities for styling.

🎯 Bonus: Full vs. Other Radius

Tailwind provides other levels of rounding too:

ClassDescription
rounded-noneNo border radius
rounded-smSmall radius
roundedDefault medium radius
rounded-mdMedium radius
rounded-lgLarge radius
rounded-xlExtra-large radius
rounded-2xl2x extra-large
rounded-3xl3x extra-large
rounded-fullFully rounded (circle)
